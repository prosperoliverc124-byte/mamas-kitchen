Our Story

Meet Mama Chioma

The heart and soul behind every dish

It all started in a small kitchen in 1998 when Mama Chioma began cooking for neighbors who couldn't resist the aroma drifting from her window. What started as plates of jollof rice shared with friends became a full restaurant loved by thousands.

Today, Mama's Kitchen serves authentic Nigerian dishes made with the same love, the same recipes, and the same fire that started it all. Every plate tells a story. Every bite takes you home.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know
Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We deliver within Lekki, Victoria Island, and Ikoyi areas. Delivery fee starts from ₦500 depending on your location. Order through our WhatsApp or call us directly.

Can I book for private events?

Absolutely! We cater for birthdays, weddings, corporate events, and private parties. Contact us at least 3 days in advance and we'll create a custom menu for your event.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 10AM to 10PM, and Sundays from 12PM to 9PM. We're closed on public holidays but available for pre-booked catering.

Do you have vegetarian options?

Yes we do! We offer vegetable soup, fried plantain, jollof rice without meat, and several other vegetarian-friendly dishes. Just let us know your preference when ordering.

How do I make a reservation?

You can reserve a table through our contact form below, send us a WhatsApp message, or call us directly at 0901 234 5678. Walk-ins are also welcome but reservations are recommended on weekends.
